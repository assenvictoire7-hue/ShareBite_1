Why ShareBite Exists

In many communities, large amount of good food are thrown away daily-from market,farms,event and restaurants-while families,orphans and the homeless go to bed hungry

ShareBite is a local redistribution system that bridges this gap. We believe that no edible food should ever reach a landfill while a neighbor is in need.

30% of global food produced is wasted anually while millions go hungry
